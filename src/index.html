About Coastal Edge Landscaping

Coastal Edge Landscaping is a locally owned Gold Coast business specialising in residential landscaping, turf installation, paving, and garden maintenance. Our Services

Landscaping Services Across the Gold Coast

At Coastal Edge Landscaping, we provide a complete range of residential landscaping services designed to improve both the look and functionality of your outdoor space. From turf installation and garden beds to paving and retaining walls, our team delivers practical solutions tailored to Gold Coast homes and conditions.

Whether you are upgrading a new build, refreshing an existing backyard, or improving drainage and levels, we take a hands-on approach to every project. We focus on clean finishes, durable materials, and layouts that are easy to maintain while still looking great year-round.

Our landscaping services are suitable for homeowners, investors, and property managers looking for reliable workmanship and clear communication. Every project is completed with attention to detail and a commitment to long-lasting results that add real value to your property.
