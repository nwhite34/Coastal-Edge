About Coastal Edge Landscaping

Coastal Edge Landscaping is a locally owned Gold Coast business specialising in residential landscaping, turf installation, paving, and garden maintenance. We work closely with homeowners to create practical, low-maintenance outdoor spaces that suit the coastal lifestyle and add long-term value to their property.

We take pride in delivering quality workmanship, honest advice, and outdoor spaces our clients can enjoy for years to come. Justin Bobley Owner & Operator
